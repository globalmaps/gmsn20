This package is Global Map version 2 (national & regional) in user friendly format.
Data were produced by National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions under their responsibilities and derived into other format by the Secretariat of ISCGM.
Metadata can be downloaded with the data in official format.

National Mapping Organization of respective countries and regions have the copyright of the data.
Please read the following the sentence about use of this data and follow the instruction.

Senegal: The Global Map of Senegal is free of charge for any purpose. However, if you intend to use the data for commercial purpose, you will need to get a license from DTGC. Please jump to DTGC Web site for licensing. Any unauthorized use of the data for any commercial purposes is in violation of international copyright laws and is strictly forbidden.
